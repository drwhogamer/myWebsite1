LoZ: Twilight Princess

This is a list of how many hidden items are in Twilight Princess and where they are located.

      8) In the Goron Mines again.

      14) Falbi's Flight-By-Fowl in lake hylia on the second platform after finding all of the light tears.

      16) In the Lakebed Temple.
      17) Also in the Lakebed Temple.

      19) In the Spirit Spring in Lake Hylia, in a room you have to clawshot to.
      20) Light two lamps to the southwest of the warp spot in a cave you bomb the blocked entrance to.

      22) Slash at a boar that is being cooked in the Orc Camp in the Gerudo Desert until it explodes.

  • Poe's Souls

    There are twenty poe's souls you have to find to get an empty bottle. Then, there are another fourty Poe's souls afterwards you can collect, afterwards you will be rewarded with an infinate ammount of 200 rupee pieces (though you will be so far in the game you might not need rupees).

  • Golden Insects

    A girl named Agitha, located in Castle Town, who "sent out invitations to an insect ball", but none of the "guests" have arrived. she wants you to retreive all 12 pairs of insects. After the first insect is given to her, Agitha will give you a wallet that can hold up to 600 rupees. For every pair of insects you bring in, (each insect pair can be found in one area and has a male and female) you will receive 100 rupees. In return for the first insect of every pair you give to her, Agitha will give you a 50 rupee piece. when all insects are returned, Agitha rewards you with a wallet capable of containing 1,000 rupees.
      1) The first pair of insects, in order of when you can get them, a male and female beetle, reside North of Faron.
      2) The second pair, pill bugs, are located in Kakariko Gorge.
      3) The third pair, ants, are located in Kakariko Village and the Graveyard.
      4) The fourth pair, grasshoppers, are located North of KAkariko Village.
      5)The fifth pair, phasmids, reside on the Great Bridge of Hylia.
      6) The sixth pair, dragonflies, are located in Zora's River/Domain.
      7) The seventh pair, butterflies, are west (east on Wii) of Castle Town.
      8) The eigth pair, stag beetles, reside North of Castle Town.
      9) The ninth pair, ladybugs, are located south of Castle Town.
